## Why Restaurants Are Massively Adopting QR Codes

The food service industry adopted QR codes faster than any other sector. What started as a health necessity has become a **competitive advantage**. QR-equipped restaurants reduce costs, improve customer experience, and collect valuable data.

## 6 Practical QR Code Uses in Restaurants

### 1. Digital Menu

The most obvious application. A QR code on each table replaces the paper menu.

**Advantages:**
- Real-time updates (daily specials, out-of-stock items)
- Zero reprinting costs
- Multilingual menu possible
- Photos and detailed descriptions

<InfoBox type="tip">
Always keep some paper menus on hand. Some customers prefer the physical format, and it's important to respect that preference.
</InfoBox>

### 2. Google Reviews Collection

A QR code at the counter or on the receipt pointing directly to your Google review page. Restaurants using this technique see their review count **multiplied by 3 on average**.

### 3. Free WiFi

No more repeating the WiFi password at every table. A WiFi QR code on the wall and customers connect by scanning.

### 4. Quick Reservations

A QR code that opens your booking page (OpenTable, your website, WhatsApp). Placed on the window, it lets passersby book without entering.

### 5. Loyalty Program

A QR code on the receipt redirecting to your digital loyalty program. No more stamp cards.

### 6. Ordering and Payment

A QR code that opens your online ordering system (takeout, delivery). Ideal for food trucks and counter-service restaurants.

## Where to Place Your QR Codes

| Location | Use | Format |
|----------|-----|--------|
| Tables | Menu, ordering | Sticker or acrylic stand |
| Counter/checkout | Google reviews, loyalty | A5 display stand |
| Window | Menu, reservations | Large sticker |
| Receipt | Reviews, loyalty | Printed on receipt |
| Takeaway packaging | Menu, social media | Sticker on bag |

A 40-seat restaurant implementing QR codes can expect:

- **$200-500/year savings** on menu printing
- **+150% Google reviews** in 3 months
- **30% reduction** in WiFi password questions to staff
- **Higher average ticket** thanks to digital menu photos
