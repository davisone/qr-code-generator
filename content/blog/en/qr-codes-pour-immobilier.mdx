## Real Estate in the QR Code Era

Real estate is a sector where **information must flow fast**. Between listings, showings, documents, and contacts, agents juggle dozens of formats. QR codes simplify everything.

## 5 Practical Applications

### 1. Smart "For Sale" Sign

A QR code on your outdoor sign gives access to the full listing: photos, floor plan, price, square footage, energy rating. Prospects passing by get all the info without calling you.

### 2. Virtual Tour

Link a QR code to a 360° tour (Matterport, YouTube). Potential buyers visit the property from their couch.

### 3. Agent Contact Card

A vCard QR code on your business cards, flyers, and signs. Potential buyers save your details in one scan — no more scribbled numbers on scraps of paper.

### 4. Property Documents

QR code to a PDF containing the energy certificate, floor plans, HOA documents. All documentation instantly accessible.

### 5. GPS Location

A location QR code on the online listing lets prospects navigate directly to the property for a showing.

## Strategic Placements

- **For Sale/For Rent sign**: large QR code (min 8cm × 8cm) readable from the sidewalk
- **Agency window**: property card with QR code to full listing
- **Flyers**: distributed in the neighborhood or in mailboxes
- **Business card**: vCard QR code on the back
- **Listing portals**: in the listing photos (some portals allow this)

## Measurable Results

Real estate agencies using QR codes on their signs report:

- **40% more views** of the full listing
- **15-20% reduction** in time to sell
- **Fewer qualifying calls** (prospects arrive better informed)
