## QR Codes: An Essential Event Tool

Whether it's a trade show, conference, wedding, or festival, QR codes simplify organization and enrich the attendee experience.

## Before the Event

### Registration and Ticketing
A QR code in your communications (email, social media, flyers) redirects to the registration page. Simple and trackable.

### Add to Calendar
An event QR code that automatically adds the date, time, and venue to the attendee's calendar. Drastically reduces no-show rates.

### Directions
A location QR code on invitations guides attendees to the venue.

## During the Event

### Smart Badge
Each badge carries the owner's vCard QR code. To exchange contacts, attendees scan each other's badges.

### Session Program
QR code to the full program as a PDF or web page. Updated in real time if a schedule changes.

### Event WiFi
Essential. WiFi QR code at the entrance and in each room.

### Exhibitor Booths
Each booth can display a QR code to its product page, LinkedIn, or a contact form.

## After the Event

### Feedback and Satisfaction
QR code to a feedback form sent by email or displayed at the exit. Immediate feedback is the most valuable.

### Replay and Resources
QR code to slides, conference videos, or shared documents.

### Post-Event Networking
QR code to a LinkedIn or WhatsApp group dedicated to attendees.

<InfoBox type="tip">
For B2B trade shows, create a vCard QR code for each salesperson at your booth. Visitors leave with the right contact saved in their phone.
</InfoBox>
