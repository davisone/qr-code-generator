## Connected Hospitality with QR Codes

QR codes have become a standard in hospitality. From check-in to check-out, they simplify every step of the stay. Here's how to integrate them into your property.

## Applications by Area

### Reception
- **WiFi QR code**: the most requested. Place it on the counter and in the welcome kit
- **Digital check-in**: link to a pre-filled form to speed up arrival
- **Hotel guide**: PDF with schedules, services, and useful numbers

### Guest Room
- **WiFi**: reminder on the nightstand or desk
- **Room service**: QR code to the menu and ordering system
- **Local info**: guide to nearby restaurants and activities
- **Feedback**: QR code to a satisfaction survey

### Common Areas
- **Restaurant/bar**: digital menu
- **Gym**: video equipment instructions
- **Pool/spa**: hours and rules

### Checkout
- **Google/TripAdvisor reviews**: QR code on the bill or at the checkout counter
- **Loyalty program**: quick signup before departure

## WiFi: The #1 Hotel QR Code

It's the number one guest request upon arrival. A WiFi QR code eliminates:
- Calls to the front desk for the password
- Typos (complex passwords)
- Guest frustration

<InfoBox type="tip">
Place the WiFi QR code in the welcome folder, on the nightstand AND in the lobby. Redundancy matters — guests don't always look in the same place.
</InfoBox>

## Impact on Guest Reviews

Hotels that make it easy to leave reviews via QR code see a significant increase in their online score. A TripAdvisor or Google QR code at reception captures guests **at peak satisfaction** (after a great stay).
