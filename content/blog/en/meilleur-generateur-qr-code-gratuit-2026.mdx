## Why Compare QR Code Generators?

Not all QR code generators are created equal. Some restrict free features, others add their logo to your QR codes, and some collect your data. Here's an honest comparison of the solutions available in 2026.

## Comparison Criteria

- **QR code types**: URL, text, WiFi, vCard, event, etc.
- **Customization**: colors, logo, shapes
- **Tracking**: scan statistics
- **Export**: available formats (PNG, SVG, PDF)
- **Limits**: number of QR codes, scans, duration
- **Pricing**: free tier vs. paid plans

- **Customization**: templates, colors, shapes, logo
