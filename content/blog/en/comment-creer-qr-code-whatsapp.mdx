## WhatsApp: Customers' Preferred Communication Channel

With **2 billion active users**, WhatsApp has become an essential communication channel for businesses. The numbers speak for themselves:

- Message open rate: **98%** (vs 20% for email)
- Average response time: **90 seconds**
- 175 million people contact a Business account every day

A WhatsApp QR code lets your customers reach you in **a single gesture**.

## How It Works

The QR code encodes a `wa.me` link that directly opens a WhatsApp conversation with your number. You can even pre-fill a message so the customer just has to press Send.

Example generated link: `https://wa.me/15551234567?text=Hello, I would like...`

## Create Your WhatsApp QR Code

### 1. Prepare Your Number

Your number must be in international format:
- US: +1 555 123 4567
- UK: +44 7911 123456
- Australia: +61 4 1234 5678

<InfoBox type="info">
The number must be linked to an active WhatsApp account (personal or Business). WhatsApp Business is recommended for professional use.
</InfoBox>

### 2. Define a Pre-filled Message (Optional but Recommended)

The pre-filled message eliminates first-contact friction. Examples:

- **Restaurant**: "Hello, I'd like to book a table for..."
- **E-commerce**: "Hello, I have a question about my order #..."
- **Service**: "Hello, I'd like a quote for..."
- **Support**: "Hello, I need help with..."

### 3. Generate and Download

Go to the [QRaft WhatsApp generator](/qr-code-generator/whatsapp), enter your number and message, then download.

## Where to Use Your WhatsApp QR Code

- **Business card**: replace "Call us" with "Message us on WhatsApp"
- **Storefront window**: "Got a question? Scan and let's chat"
- **Product packaging**: "Customer support via WhatsApp"
- **Flyers and brochures**: for local marketing campaigns
- **Website**: complementing the WhatsApp button on your contact page

## WhatsApp Personal vs WhatsApp Business

| Feature | Personal | Business |
|---------|----------|---------|
| QR code | Works | Works |
| Auto-messages | No | Yes |
| Product catalog | No | Yes |
| Contact labels | No | Yes |
| Statistics | No | Yes |
| Multi-device | Limited | Up to 5 |

For professional use, **WhatsApp Business** (free) is strongly recommended.

## Tips to Maximize Conversations

1. **Respond quickly**: WhatsApp creates an expectation of fast replies. If you can't respond 24/7, set up an auto-reply
2. **Customize the pre-filled message** based on where the QR code is displayed
3. **Display your hours** next to the QR code: "WhatsApp available 9am to 6pm"
